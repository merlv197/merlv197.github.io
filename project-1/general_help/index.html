Welcome to Stairs

Your journey begins when selecting a shape in the upper left corner menu.

Each shape corresponds to a type of action performed on the stairs, in the real world.

For example, the circle represents people.

Once you choose your shape, you will have the choice between two riddles. These riddles are asking you to reveal some kind of information, this piece of information is hidden in one of the larger shapes.

But you can only reveal this information if you solve the riddle using the plus and minus sign, hence changing the crowd on the stairs.

You can always reset by clicking on the shape you chose in the menu, or by selecting another shape at any time.
